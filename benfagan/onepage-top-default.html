Ben Fagan Designs

Benjamin Fagan Designs is a South Lake Tahoe residential design firm established in 1975. With 40+ years of experience, Ben Fagan has designed a multitude of homes ranging from small chalets, to lakefront mansions, to steep mountain-side residences. Ben's work is recognized for creative use of materials, intriguing roof lines, and great spatial arrangements. The firm is dedicated to prompt but thorough design process, embraces communication between owner and designer, and eases a smooth permit process. Ben is frequently recommended by previous vlients, builders, and realtors. He is well known in the town for the quality of his work and the commitment of his team to the successful completion of every project. Discover your home in Tahoe!
